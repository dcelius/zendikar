Welcome to Zendikar

An exploration and adventure-focused D&D 5e campaign setting based on the wild plane of Zendikar from Magic: the Gathering.

Trap-riddled ruins can be found on every continent. Mysterious glyphs hint at truths long forgotten. Unspeakable monsters lurk in the quiet of these hidden monuments of a forgotten past. The ruins still emanate power, and both planeswalkers and local explorers alike will undergo great peril to reap their rewards. From the otherworldly hedrons that drift across the sky, to the wondrous power of Life Blooms, Zendikar has no shortage of adventure and mysteries. While the Roil prevents civilization from grabbing hold, the spirit of the people of Zendikar knows no bounds as they continue to dredge into the earth, searching for ancient secrets perhaps better left undiscovered.

Races of Zendikar

A wide variety of peoples walk the plane of Zendikar, including Elves, Goblins, Humans, Kor, Merfolk, and Vampires - among others. Many of these races have custom stats for you to use, but if you prefer to just reflavor existing content, alternatives are provided.

Elves

Being very adaptable, it's no wonder the elves of Zendikar flourish in places where other races struggle. Most commonly found on Murasa and Bala Ged, elves are split into 3 distinct nations and many distant clans.

Goblins

Adventurous and brave to the point of stupidity, Zendikari goblins are natural explorers of ancient ruins. Split into 4 tribes, goblins can be found all across Zendikar.

  • Details

Humans

The most numerous race on Zendikar, humans can be found on every continent - but Zendikar's wild nature means proper settlements are far and few between.

  • Details

Kor

Born nomads and master climbers, the Kor are a race of people deeply tied to the land of Zendikar. People of few words, the kor use a form of nonverbal communication.

  • Details

Merfolk

Curious and analytical, Zendikari merfolk are natural scholars and historians. Split into three creeds based on faith, merfolk can be found all across Zendikar.

  • Details

Vampires

Afflicted by their eldritch curse, Zendikari vampires live on Guul Draz and live a life of decadence and debauchery.

A new world means new rules. Campaign guidelines, rules tweaks, and new mechanics all can be found here. Many of the rule changes and new mechanics have been inspired by OneD&D playtest documents, Pathfinder 2e, and Baldur's Gate 3.

World of Zendikar

The expansive and wild plane of Zendikar contains 7 continents: Akoum, Bala Ged, Guul Draz, Murasa, Ondu, Sejiri, and Tazeem. With vastly different ecosystems, mysteries left unsolved, and ruins still undiscovered, each continent has its secrets. And they will not give up their secrets lightly.
